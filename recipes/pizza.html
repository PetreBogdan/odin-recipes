How to Make Pizza on the Grill

No pizza oven? No problem! Make your homemade pizza on the grill for that
classic smoky, wood fired oven flavor. And even if you have a pizza oven, you might
prefer the grill from time to time.

This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted
red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Of course, you can use whatever
toppings you like!
